Welcome to Fariba Studio.
Based in East Coast Canada and inspired by the world, I find beauty in the quiet
details; the way light hits a mountain range, the nostalgia of a seaside stroll or the playful spirit of a loyal pet.

Using traditional hand-painted watercolor techniques, I create original works and greeting cards designed to bring a sense of serenity to your space.
